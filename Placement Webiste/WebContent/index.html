About University

Jamia Millia Islamia, a Central University, renowned for its highly glorious past and an equally promising future, has more than 23,000 students 800+ full time teaching faculty, 9 faculties, 39 departments and more than 200 courses. Jamia Millia Islamia (translation: National Islamic University) is a public central universityin Delhi. It was established during British rule in 1920. It became a Central University by an act of the Indian Parliament in 1988.

• The University is placed at 12th position among the Universities and 6th position among the central Universities of the country.

• The University is ranked in 800- 1000 bracket in world ranking by Times Higher Education.

About Department


Department of Computer Science is one of the youngest departments of Faculty of Natural Sciences, Jamia Millia Islamia, one of the most renowned universities of the country.

Established in 1999 with a vision to train and develop highly skilled human resource in the field of computer science and bioinformatics and to conduct core and applied research in the thrust areas of computer science.


The emphasis is on creating an environment for students to explore, experiment, discover and realize their potential. The prime most event hosted by the department is the annual fest, Teknothon.
